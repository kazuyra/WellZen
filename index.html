FAQs

Is this medical advice?

No. This site is for education only. For personal concerns, speak with a parent/guardian and a licensed medical professional.

Where do you get your information?

We summarize guidance from reputable sources (CDC, NIH, WHO, AAP, Mayo Clinic) and cite references in each article.

Can schools use these resources?

Yes. All content is free to share with attribution. Contact us to request custom bundles.
