How to Start Eating Better?

Simple, school-day nutrition strategies you can actually stick to.

The “1-2-3 plate”

  • 1 protein: eggs, yogurt, chicken, tofu, beans.
  • 2 colors: fruits/veggies—berries, carrots, spinach, peppers.
  • 3 smart carbs/fats: oats, whole-grain wrap, rice, nuts, olive oil.

Snack upgrade ideas

  • Greek yogurt + frozen fruit
  • Apple + peanut butter
  • Trail mix + water bottle
  • Hummus + baby carrots
