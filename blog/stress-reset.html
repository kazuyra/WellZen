Tips on Resetting Stress

Short, science-backed techniques to calm your body and refocus your mind.

3 fast resets

  1. 4-7-8 breathing: Inhale 4, hold 7, exhale 8 for 3–4 cycles.
  2. Physiological sigh: Two quick inhales through the nose, long exhale through the mouth.
  3. 30-second reset: Look at something far away; relax jaw/shoulders; slow exhale.
