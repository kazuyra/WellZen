Our Story

Empowering students with evidence-based wellness knowledge

HabitTide is a student-led initiative bringing science-backed health insights to teens in a relatable, simple, and trustworthy way — because understanding your body shouldn't require a medical degree.

Why I started HabitTide

As a high school student passionate about healthcare and technology, as well as being co-president of a club that focuses on teaching concepts which regular classes may skip over, I decided to create this website focused on science-based wellness information in a format made for education.

HabitTide combines medical research, digital education, and student perspective to help young people build healthier habits and stronger futures. Focused in all perspectives: academically, mentally, and physically.

My mission is to make credible health knowledge free, accessible, actionable, and inspiring.
