How to verify health information

Check the source

Prefer government, medical institutions, and peer-reviewed science.

Beware social media myths

Health advice online can be misleading — always double-check.

Look for citations

Credible info links to research, not opinions or trends.

Consult professionals

Talk to healthcare providers for medical advice and decisions.
